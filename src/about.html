Alessio Galdi
Keep in Touch Instagram, Email, Phone Keep in Touch

Award-winning Creative Director based in Rome, with over 10 years of experience in branding, strategic design, and corporate identity.

He works on concept-driven design with a multi-channel approach across a wide range of industries. Over time, he has received numerous international accolades, including the prestigious Yellow Pencil at the D&AD Awards, where he also serves as a judge in the Branding category. He is also a Cannes Lions winner, further cementing his reputation for creative excellence.

At Landor, he leads teams and clients on both local and global projects for brands such as Elica, Imperia, Orchestra Sinfonica di Milano, Milano Cortina 2026 Olympics, P&G, Kellogg's, Inter Milano, NeN, Campari Group, Banca Mediolanum, Scarpa, Presa, Lavazza, WPP, Generali, Enel, Enel X, Spotify, Polaroid, Saipem, and Valtellina, among many others.
